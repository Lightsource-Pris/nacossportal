About Us


NACOSS which stands for Nigerian Association of Computer Science Students is one of the oldest associations in the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB). NACOSS generally is one of the present association in most universities in Nigeria as a result of it's reputable standards and dedication by her leaders. This is expressed in her slogan "Networking The World". The standards have been followed and improved upon from teams to teams in different institutions and collectively.
The FUNAAB Department is currently headed by Professor Olusegun Folurunso
